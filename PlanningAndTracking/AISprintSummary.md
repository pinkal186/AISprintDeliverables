## Evaluation Philosophy

### Six Operating Principles
1. **Problem before solution** - Establish need before architecture
2. **Productive failure** - Build incomplete baseline to understand constraints
3. **Self-explanation** - Know why each decision exists
4. **Research before summaries** - Read original papers first
5. **Construction before observation** - Build, test, defend, revise
6. **Transfer before mastery** - Apply to new contexts

### Success Criteria
- Working implementation is necessary but not sufficient
- Must understand problem, defend architecture, measure failures
- Can adapt ideas to unseen problems
- Evidence-based completion over AI agent assessment
- Demonstrate thinking system, not just final solutions
